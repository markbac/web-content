About tarf.co.uk

tarf.co.uk is a resource hub designed to share knowledge, tools, and insights across various domains, including software architecture, technology, and engineering. This site serves as a repository for professionals and enthusiasts looking for glossaries, reference materials, templates, and guides.

The name "Tarf" originates from Beta Cancri, the brightest star in the constellation Cancer. With a strong interest in astronomy and physics, I wanted to tie this passion to the site's identity. While astronomy is grounded in science and discovery (unlike astrology, which is nonsense), the star Tarf represents the blend of exploration and knowledge that this site aspires to provide.

As the site grows, more resources and tools will be added to support architects, engineers, and technology enthusiasts. Check back often to see what's new!
